About

Beta Chi Pi is a science honors society at Parkway West High, similar to Mu Alpha Theta (if you've ever head of that club).

Led by Mrs. Gallaher, the AP Chemistry teacher, and a comittee of student officers, Beta Chi Pi strives to let its members to share and learn about chemistry, biology, and physics.

This club will be both academically and creatively fun, and most importantly, will be filled with really cool students which you can learn and befriend. Let's have a great year!
