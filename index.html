About

Beta Chi Pi is a science honors society at Parkway West High, similar to Mu Alpha Theta (if you've ever head of that club).

Led by Mrs. Gallaher, the AP Chemistry teacher, and a committee of student officers, Beta Chi Pi strives to let its members to share and learn about chemistry, biology, and physics.

By attending meetings, tutoring, helping out teachers, teaching, and more, members earn a certain amount of points with each task. In order to graduate this society, memebers need to have acheived a certain amount of points. The amount of points per task and needed to graduate will be decided later.

This club will be both academically and creatively fun, and most importantly, will be filled with really cool students which you can learn and befriend. Let's have a great year!
